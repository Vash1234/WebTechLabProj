Make a difference

1.Adopt not shop -  adopt from an animal shelter, this way you can help prevent puppy mills. Adopting helps more than one life, this way you make room for other animals to stay in the shelter. 
2.Spread the word - raise awareness about animal cruelty 
3. Volunteer - given the chance, help in any way you can. volunteer in animal welfare organizations 
4. Donate - animal welfare organizations needs all the help they can get, donating can help provide what they need

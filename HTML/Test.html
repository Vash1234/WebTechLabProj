Animal Abuse Statistics

According to report on november 2012, PAWS(Philippine Animal Welfare Society) receives about 40 calls about animal abuse daily. There are over 100 cases of animal cruelty annually and many of which are never reported. Pet-abuse.com has tracked 25 years of animal abuse and has found reports over 19,000 cases. But again these reports are made by humans, there could be hundreds, thousands more of animal cruelty that remains unreported.
